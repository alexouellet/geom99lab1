Top of the Giant, Sleeping Giant Provincial Park

This photo above was from when I visited the Top of the Giant in August 2021. With an elevation gain of 290 m and a round trip distance of 22.4 km, you better come prepared with food and water!

The dynamic map below shows the straight line path that a boat would take from the Top of the Giant, to Prince Arthur's Landing (a harbour in Thunder Bay, Ontario)

A straight line boat ride from the Top of the Giant (like the map below displays) would only be a 25 km trip, however walking from Top of The Giant to Prince Arthurs Landing is almost 87 km. Maybe they should cosider creating a boat shuttle service!
